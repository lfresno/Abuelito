# Abuelito
"Abuelito" will be a walking simulator game, which uses some FPS mechanics. In this game, the player will control Manuel, an old man who will walk through his beutiful garden and water his plants with a water gun. This simple gameplay will guide the player though ManuelÂ´s history.
